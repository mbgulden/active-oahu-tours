Please notify us more than 48 hours in advance for a 100% refund. You may alternately re-schedule your tour for another day.

Re-scheduled tours cannot receive refunds.

**You will be charged the full amount if you cancel after 48 hours**, unless you have purchased [cancellation insurance](https://activeoahutours.com/trip-cancellation-insurance-terms-and-conditions).
