#### **1. E-Bike and Kayak Tour to Offshore Islands in Kailua**

Combining the thrill of cruising through town on an electric bike with the unforgettable adventure of kayaking to offshore islands, Active Oahu’s e-bike and kayak tours are a one-of-a-kind experience you won’t want to miss! Our tours give you the flexibility to adventure on your own with a self-guided experience or be led by one of our expert and lifeguard certified guides with our guided adventures! Whether you are a solo explorer, couple, family, or group of friends, we have an adventure to fit your needs.
Every tour includes an electric bike rental equipped with a kayak trailer so that you can avoid the hassle of parking and spend more time making unforgettable memories in Hawaii. After cruising through town to the beach, you can paddle to Popoia Island for a shorter family-friendly adventure or the Mokulua Islands for a longer, more advanced adventure. These islands are known for their surrounding crystal clear water, seabird sanctuaries, and stunning coastal views. As you paddle you might even spot Hawaiian Green Sea Turtles, Hawaiian Monk Seals, and schools of tropical fish.
Learn more about our self-guided and guided adventures here: [Tour Information and Options](https://activeoahutours.com/activities/)

#### **2. Snorkel on the West Side at Electric Beach**

Tucked away on Oahu’s West Side lies a hidden gem known as Electric Beach or its official name, Kahe Point. Often overlooked by tourists, this vibrant coastal spot offers a unique and thrilling snorkel experience. Warm water from a nearby powerplant attracts an incredible variety of sealife, often visited by sea turtles, tropical fish, and even the occasional pod of dolphins. 
At Active Oahu we offer complete snorkel set rentals if you want to go explore on your own, or the option of a guided tour led by one of our expert lifeguard certified adventure guides that will take you right to the best spots. 
To rent snorkel gear sets click here: [Snorkel Mask and Fin Rentals](https://activeoahutours.com/rentals/oahu-snorkel-mask-and-fin-rentals/)
To learn more about our guided snorkel tour at Electric Beach click here: [West Oahu Guided Snorkel Tour ](https://activeoahutours.com/activities/west-oahu-guided-snorkel-tour/)

#### **3. Learn to Surf on South Shore**

Hawaii is known for its incredible surf locations, and when you visit the island, you can try it out for yourself by taking a surf lesson on the south shore near Waikiki! Surf HNL is known for its 5-star surf lessons and makes safety their highest priority. Perfect for beginners, get ready to catch your first waves with the expert guides at Surf HNL. Their lessons are conducted at one of the most scenic and safest beaches on Oahu, giving you peace of mind as you paddle out. Their tours are a family friendly experience and they teach kids as young as three years old. Ready to surf like a pro? Make your booking with Surf HNL today! 
Visit this link to learn more about the lessons Surf HNL offers: [Surf HNL Lessons](https://www.surfhnl.com/lessons)

#### **4. Lanikai E-Bike, Snorkel, and Pillbox Hike Self-Guided Adventure**

If you are looking for the ultimate adventure on land and in Hawaii’s stunning waters, look no further than Active Oahu’s Lanikai e-bike, snorkel and pillbox hike self-guided adventure! You’ll begin by loading up all your gear at our shop where our adventure guides will help you out and give you tips and tricks before you head out for this unique adventure. 
This adventure includes an electric bike, complete snorkel gear set, and directions on how to get to each of the locations. You’ll begin your journey by cruising through town to the Lanikai Pillbox Hike. This 30-minute climb to the top will reward you with stunning panoramic views of the coastline and the opportunity to explore abandoned pillboxes from World War II. After the hike, cruise down to Lanikai Beach and cool off by snorkeling in the crystal clear waters. Discover vibrant coral reefs, schools of fish, and Hawaiian Green Sea Turtles as you swim around and soak up the sun!
Visit this page to learn more about this adventure: [Lanikai E-bike, Snorkel, and Pillbox Hike Self Guided Adventure](https://activeoahutours.com/oahu-kayaking-and-beach-adventures/lanikai-e-bike-snorkel-and-pillbox-hike-self-guided-adventure/)

#### **5. Shark Diving Adventure on the North Shore**

Calling all adrenaline junkies and those looking for an incredible ocean experience! Shark diving adventures with One Ocean Diving give you the opportunity to swim in the water with a variety of sharks- no cage involved! With rave reviews and a 100% safety record, One Ocean Diving is the crew you want to take you to see some sharks and other sea life! Their tours give you an hour in the water with the sharks with certified safety divers to show you the way and give you peace of mind throughout the experience. Shark Diving is a bucket list experience you won’t want to miss on your trip to Hawaii!
Learn more and make a booking with One Ocean Diving here: [One Ocean Diving ](https://oneoceandiving.com/book-now)
