#### **A World Famous Beach Worth the Trip**

Lanikai Beach is known around the world for its powder-soft sand, crystal clear waters, and incredible coastal views. It’s also a launch point for water adventures like snorkeling among tropical fish and hawaiian green sea turtles and kayaking through the turquoise water. Whether you are looking for a thrilling experience on the water or want to relax on the shore and soak up the sun with a cooler full of drinks and snacks, Active Oahu is here to make all your beach day dreams a reality by offering beach gear rentals and self-guided and guided kayaking tours to offshore islands.

#### **Transportation to the Beach**

There are two main ways to get to Lanikai Beach depending on what activities you are interested in:

- Electric Bikes: renting electric bikes is the most adventurous and convenient way to get to the beach because you won’t have to sit in traffic or battle for a parking space. You can also rent a kayak trailer for your electric bike and effortlessly tow a kayak down to the beach. This is also a good option for those who don’t have a vehicle of their own. #### **Where to Park**

The beach access for Lanikai is located in a neighborhood, making parking limited. If you want to find street parking, we recommend going early in the morning. If you rent e-bikes you don’t have to worry about parking and can lock them up at Lanikai Park near the beach access. Alternatively, you can park at Kailua Beach Park and take the scenic walking path over to Lanikai Beach.

### **Activities at Lanikai Beach**

#### **Incredible Snorkeling**

Lanikai Beach is the perfect place for beginner and advanced snorkelers alike. With calm, clear, and shallow water you can have peace of mind as you explore the unique underwater world that lies beneath the surface. As you snorkel you’ll discover vibrant coral reefs, a variety of tropical fish, and if you are lucky you may even spot Hawaiian green sea turtles gliding among the reef.

#### **Kayaking to Offshore Islands**

One of the best adventures on the island is kayaking to the offshore islands and exploring. At Active Oahu we offer both self-guided and guided tours to best fit the needs of you and your group.

- Kayak to Popoia Island: Popoia Island is a short paddle to an island that also acts as a seabird sanctuary, making it the perfect adventure for families and those who are new to kayaking. You’ll glide through the clear waters and enjoy incredible views all around as you paddle and once you reach the island. After reaching the island you can continue the adventure by paddling around Kailua Bay. Learn more about kayaking to Popoia Island here: [Popoia Island Kayaking Adventure](https://activeoahutours.com/oahu-kayaking-and-beach-adventures/popoia-island-kayaking-adventure/)

- Kayak to the Mokulua Islands: Also known as Kailua’s twin islands, the Mokulua Islands are a great option for those looking for a longer adventure, as it takes about an hour to paddle out to the islands. Once you reach them you can explore the tidepools, take a dip in the Queen’s Bath, and even snorkel off the shore. You’ll have incredible views of the ocean and mountain range you can only see from the islands. Learn more about kayaking to the Mokulua Islands here: [Mokulua Islands Self Guided Kayak Adventure](https://activeoahutours.com/oahu-kayaking-and-beach-adventures/mokulua-islands-self-guided-kayak-adventure/)

#### **Lanikai Pillbox Hike**

Another adventure near Lanikai Beach is the Lanikai Pillbox Hike. This moderate level hike takes about 30 minutes to reach the summit. From there you’ll be rewarded with stunning panoramic views of the coastline and offshore islands while you explore the pillboxes that were used for surveillance during World War II.

#### **Nearby Places to Eat**

After a day at Lanikai Beach, you’ll probably want to grab a bite to eat from one of the local eateries in town. Here are some of our favorites:

- Kono’s: Known for its mouthwatering kalua pork dishes and tasty milkshakes, this is a must stop place after a day of adventuring.

- Steak Rave: a local favorite and hole-in-the-wall, steak rave offers takeout plates which is perfect for a picnic on the beach. We recommend the steak plate with rice and salad.

- Sunrise Shack: For a light and refreshing treat try Sunrise Shack which offers picture perfect acai bowls and smoothies.
