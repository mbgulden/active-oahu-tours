#### **Give Your Beach Gear a Second Life**

You came to Oahu for the sun, surf, and sand, and purchased gear to make the most of your time on the island, but by the end of your vacation you may be wondering what to do with the gear before you head home? It’s common for visitors to accumulate beach gear while on vacation here on the Hawaiian islands, and sadly, a lot of that gear goes to waste after people leave. Rather than tossing your perfectly good gear in the trash, consider donating it to Active Oahu where it can have a second life.

#### **Give Back to the Island**

At Active Oahu, we are passionate about protecting our islands, minimizing waste, and making outdoor adventures accessible and sustainable. Here is where you can help! When you donate your items to us, you are not only keeping Oahu clean and clutter-free, but you are also helping the next visitors and locals enjoy the island with your gear!

- Reduce Waste: Hawaii struggles with the environmental impacts of tourism. Donating your gear is a simple way to make your trip more sustainable.

- Support Our Community: Locals and visitors alike will enjoy your gear long after you leave your island getaway.

- Convenient Drop Off: Located in Kailua, or location is easily accessible and the process is quick to make a drop off. Additionally, if you need to store some luggage with us until your flight takes off, we offer a luggage storage service through Bounce.
