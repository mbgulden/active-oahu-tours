At Active Oahu, we offer a fun, convenient, and one-of-a-kind way to experience Oahu by renting electric bikes. Kailua is the perfect beach town for e-biking because of all the local adventures that are accessible by bike. Kailua is also made with bikers in mind, with plenty of bike lanes and bike paths that will get you anywhere you want to go while keeping safety a priority.

![E-bike rentals outside of Active Oahu in Kailua](/images/placeholder-tour.jpg)

## **About Our E-Bikes**

We offer two different models of E-bikes for rent from the Electric Bike Company. Our bench seat bikes are sporty and ultra maneuverable. Our comfort seat bikes offer comfortable cruising with a basket attached to the back to carry all the gear you need for your adventure. Both models have a maximum speed of 20 miles an hour with a thumb throttle and the ability to increase or decrease the boost from the motor. Our bikes are easy to use and our adventure guides will give you a brief tutorial on how to operate them when you arrive at our shop. You’ll have the opportunity to ride them around and practice to gain your confidence before leaving the shop if you are new to riding e-bikes. We also have dry bags, snorkel gear, and other beach gear you can rent to bring with you for the day.

## **Places to E-Bike**

- Lanikai Pillbox Hike: An easy-moderate level hike that rewards you with epic panoramic views of the coastline off of Lanikai Beach. A must-do for any Oahu itinerary. Visit this page to learn more: [Lanikai Pillbox Hike Adventure Guide](https://activeoahutours.com/oahu-kayaking-and-beach-adventures/lanikai-pillbox-hike-adventure-guide/)

- Manoa Chocolate Factory: Stop in for a complimentary chocolate tasting of locally sourced and made-in-shop chocolate.

- Kalama Beach: Add a board carrier attachment to your e-bike rental and cruise over to Kailua’s iconic bodyboarding beach. Perfect for families and beginner bodyboarders to catch some waves. Visit this page to learn more: [Everything You Need to Know About Bodyboarding at Kalama Beach](https://activeoahutours.com/oahu-kayaking-and-beach-adventures/kalama-beach-bodyboarding-adventure/)

- Kailua Beach: The ultimate adventure stop that leads to more activities. Visit this page to learn everything you need to know about why you should visit Kailua Beach: [Ultimate Guide to Kailua Beach – Oahu’s Safest and Most Adventurous Beach](https://activeoahutours.com/oahu-kayaking-and-beach-adventures/ultimate-guide-for-kailua-beach-park-experience-windward-oahus-safest-and-most-adventurous-beach/)

- Lanikai Beach: This beach is world-famous for a reason. With its powdery sand, crystal clear waters, and stunning views of the Mokulua Islands, it’s easy to see why this is a must-visit beach while visiting Oahu. Additionally, it offers the perfect place for snorkeling with plenty of coral reef and sea life to discover. Check out this page to learn more about Lanikai Beach: [Ultimate Guide to Lanikai Beach](https://activeoahutours.com/oahu-kayaking-and-beach-adventures/ultimate-guide-to-lanikai-beach/)

- Local eateries and shops: Kailua not only an adventure hub, but also a foodies dream! Check out Kono’s for mouth-watering kalua pork dishes, Steak Rave for a hole-in-the-wall local favorite, Sunrise Shack for acai bowls and smoothies, Bob’s Pizzeria for boston-style pizza, or Please Come Again for locally made ice cream.

- Add a kayak trailer and tow a kayak to the Kailua Beach to gain access to Popoia Island and the Mokulua Islands. Visit this page to learn more about towing kayaks with an e-bike: [Guide to Towing Kayaks with E-Bikes in Kailua](https://activeoahutours.com/oahu-kayaking-and-beach-adventures/guide-to-towing-kayaks-with-e-bikes-in-kailua/)
