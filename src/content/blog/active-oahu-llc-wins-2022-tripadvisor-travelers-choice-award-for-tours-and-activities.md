**Laie, Hawaii – July 28, 2022 –** Active Oahu, LLC today announced it has been recognized by Tripadvisor as a 2022 Travelers’ Choice award winner for Tours and Activities.  The award celebrates businesses that have received great traveler reviews from travelers around the globe on Tripadvisor over the last 12 months. As challenging as the past year was, Active Oahu stood out by consistently delivering positive experiences to travelers.

“It feels great to look back on these reviews and know that so many visitors had excellent experiences and created fond memories,” said Michael Gulden, Founder of Active Oahu, LLC.  “When I started Active Oahu, I wanted to share the beauty I saw and experienced on Oahu and make it more accessible. I look forward to sharing even more adventures with visitors and locals alike.”

“Congratulations to the 2022 Tripadvisor Travelers’ Choice Winners,” said Kanika Soni, Chief Commercial Officer at Tripadvisor. “The Travelers’ Choice Awards recognize the best in tourism and hospitality, according to those who matter most: your guests. Ranking among the Travelers’ Choice winners is always tough — but never more so than this year as we emerge from the pandemic. Whether it’s using new technology, implementing safety measures, or hiring outstanding staff, I’m impressed by the steps you’ve taken to meet travelers’ new demands. You’ve adapted brilliantly in the face of adversity.”

**About Active Oahu, LLC**

Active Oahu, LLC was founded by Michael Gulden to make it a breeze for visitors and locals to explore Oahu by Kayak through rental delivery services and pre-planned self-guided experiences. For visitors staying longer, Active Oahu has multi-day rental packages for kayaks, beach chairs, umbrellas and other beach gear. Travelers have been using Active Oahu’s services since 2013. Active Oahu’s delivery services have expanded every year and they look forward to serving more locations and providing easy access to new adventures.

Active Oahu, LLC also owns and operates [www.activeoahu.com](http://www.activeoahu.com), a platform to help visitors to Oahu plan their Oahu adventure. There are hundreds of Oahu activities listed there as well as sights to see and adventure blog posts to explore.

**About Tripadvisor**

Tripadvisor, the world’s largest travel guidance platform*, helps hundreds of millions of people each month** become better travelers, from planning to booking to taking a trip. Travelers across the globe use the Tripadvisor site and app to discover where to stay, what to do, and where to eat based on guidance from those who have been there before. With more than 988 million reviews and opinions from nearly 8 million businesses, travelers turn to Tripadvisor to find deals on accommodations, book experiences, reserve tables at delicious restaurants, and discover great places nearby. As a travel guidance company available in 43 markets and 22 languages, Tripadvisor makes planning easy no matter the trip type.
