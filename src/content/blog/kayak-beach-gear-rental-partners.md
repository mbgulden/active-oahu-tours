At Active Oahu Tours, we believe in providing our clients with an unparalleled customer experience. We can deliver to most vacation rentals on the island, whether your property is on the North Shore or you have multiple properties across the island. We strive to provide your guests with seamless, hassle-free experiences so they can focus on enjoying their vacations and their stays at your property.

Our partners trust us to provide rental gear delivery services straight to their vacation rental locations. We can deliver beach chairs, umbrellas, snorkel masks, fin sets, life vests, boogie boards, surfboards, stand-up paddleboards, and kayaks right to your door. With multiple pickup and drop-off times, guests can customize when they receive their equipment that works with their check-in and check-out dates and times.
