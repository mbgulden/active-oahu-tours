Whether you are arriving early before a hotel check-in, checking out with hours to spare before a flight, or just want to explore the area without towing your bags around, the question always arises: what do we do with all our stuff? Good news! Active Oahu has an affordable and convenient solution for you. We’ve partnered with Bounce, a leading luggage storage company, to offer secure, affordable, and flexible storage right in the heart of Kailua Town.

![A sign advertising Bounce Luggage Service with Active Oahu sits on top of a counter next to Bounce luggage tags.](/images/placeholder-tour.jpg)

## **How it Works**

At Active Oahu we provide a safe and secure place to store your luggage or other items for as little as a few hours or up to multiple days. To make your booking simply:

- Download the Bounce app and select our location “Near Kailua Farmers’ Market Storage Spot”. It will have a cover photo of our storefront, Active Oahu. 

- Select “book this store” and input how many bags you want to check in and what types. Checkout using a credit card, paypal, or apple pay. 

- Once making the booking you’ll receive a QR code you’ll show to our employees at Active Oahu when you arrive to check in your bags. 

- We will place your luggage in a secure area where it will be protected until your designated pickup time.

 It’s as simple as that!

## **Why Use Bounce Through Active Oahu**

Bounce insures all of your luggage up to $10,000 with no extra fees, giving you peace of mind knowing your luggage is safe with us no matter what. There is also a free cancellation policy up to one hour before your drop off time, giving you flexibility with your schedule and any changes that come up. Storing your items with us via bounce is also an affordable option.

- Small purses and tote bags cost $4.90 per day

- Suitcases and backpacks cost $6.90 per day

- Larger items such as surfboards, bikes, and golf bags cost $9.90 per day

Our location is especially ideal if you are staying near or in Kailua and going on adventures to places like Lanikai Beach, Kalama Beach, and the Lanikai Pillbox Hike. Whether you are a visitor trying to make the most of your last moments on Oahu or a local adventurer heading out on a weekend trip, Bounce storage with Active Oahu provides a way for you to accomplish all you want to, without being weighed down with your luggage.
