#### **Where to Use the Gear**

Wherever you are staying on the island, there are incredible adventures to be had with your beach gear rentals. Here are some of our recommendations:

- Paddle to Chinaman’s Hat: Take your kayak or paddleboard rentals over to Kualoa Regional Park and paddle out to the offshore island known as Chinaman’s Hat. Once you land on the island you can hike to the top for stunning panoramic views of the Koolau Mountain Range and coastline. Learn more about paddling to Chinaman’s Hat here: [Chinamans Hat Kayak Adventure](https://activeoahutours.com/oahu-kayaking-and-beach-adventures/chinamans-hat-kayak-adventure/)

- Kayak to the Kaneohe Sandbar: Glide through the crystal clear waters and keep an eye out for Hawaiian green sea turtles as you paddle out to the sandbar where you will have the surreal experience of standing up in the middle of the ocean. This is also a great spot for snorkeling as there are many coral reefs along the way. Learn more about kayaking to the Kaneohe Sandbar here: [Kaneohe Sandbar Self Guided Kayak Adventure](https://activeoahutours.com/oahu-kayaking-and-beach-adventures/kaneohe-sandbar-self-guided-kayak-adventure/)

- Paddle to the Mokulua Islands: For those looking for a longer adventure out on the water, Kailua’s Twin Islands are the perfect paddle! Located about an hour’s paddle from Kailua Beach, this thrilling adventure will land you on the Mokulua Islands where you can explore tide pools and even the famous “Queen’s Bath”. Learn more about kayaking to the Mokulua Islands here: [Mokulua Islands Self Guided Kayak Adventure](https://activeoahutours.com/oahu-kayaking-and-beach-adventures/mokulua-islands-self-guided-kayak-adventure/)

- Bodyboard at Kalama Beach: Load up all your beach gear and head to Kalama Beach for a day of catching waves and hanging out under the sun! We recommend bringing a cooler with drinks and food, beach chairs and an umbrella, and of course, boogie boards! Learn more about bodyboarding at Kalama Beach here: [Kalama Beach Bodyboarding Adventure](https://activeoahutours.com/oahu-kayaking-and-beach-adventures/kalama-beach-bodyboarding-adventure/)

- Snorkel Around the Island: There are tons of great snorkel spots around the island to check out with your multi-day snorkel rentals. We recommend snorkeling at Shark’s Cove or Lanikai Beach for beginners and Electric Beach for more advanced snorkelers! Learn more about the best snorkel spots on Oahu here: 

- E-bike around Kailua Town: Rent e-bikes and experience all the adventures Kailua has to offer while cruising around on two wheels. e-bikes will give you easy access to the most popular spots and you won’t have to battle for parking. Learn more about our e-bike rentals here: [E-bike Rentals in Kailua](https://activeoahutours.com/oahu-kayaking-and-beach-adventures/e-bike-rentals-in-kailua/)
