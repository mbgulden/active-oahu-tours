#### **The Ultimate Lanikai Beach Experience**

Get ready to embark on a one-of-a-kind adventure that combines the thrill of riding an electric bike with the beauty and wonder of snorkeling in the ocean. Our E-Bike and Snorkel Self-Guided Tour takes you along scenic coastal bike paths, followed by snorkeling in the crystal clear turquoise waters of Lanikai beach. Since it is self-guided, you are free to pedal at your own pace and stop to enjoy the sights along the way! Our tour includes everything you need to ensure a stress-free and unforgettable experience. Whether you are an avid snorkeler or a first-time e-bike rider, this tour is perfect for adventurers of all levels.

![visitors overlooking a scenic ocean view as they e-bike on a bike path](https://activeoahutours.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/Active-Oahu-E-Bikes-127-1024x683.jpg)

#### **Beginning the Adventure**

Your adventure begins at our storefront in Kailua. Since you will be exploring using the e-bikes there is no need for a car so adventurers can be dropped off or you can leave your vehicle parked outside our store while you are exploring. Remember to bring reef-safe sunscreen, towels, sunglasses, and anything else you might need during the duration of your adventure. Forget anything? No problem! We have a variety of beach necessities as well as cold beverages available for purchase at our storefront so that you can pick up any last minute essentials before heading out.

Upon arriving, meet us at our check-in desk where you will sign waivers and our adventure guides will get you and your crew fitted with e-bikes, helmets, and snorkel gear. We’ll also give you a quick briefing on the safe operation of the bikes and share some general guidelines and tips for your tour to ensure you have the best experience possible. Our guides can also provide recommendations of other stops you may enjoy as you explore such as restaurants, shops, and cool spots to take photos! With all the bikes set up and gear loaded, you’ll be ready to start your tour!

#### **Arriving at Lanikai Beach**

Lanikai beach is just a quick ten minute bike ride away. Enjoy the scenery as you ride along some of the most stunning landscapes Kailua has to offer. You’ll cruise through the Enchanted Lakes neighborhood and use bike lanes/paths as you enjoy panoramic views of the lush green landscape. Feel free to stop and take pictures along the way. You will first navigate to Lanikai Park, where bike racks are conveniently located on the perimeter of the park near the baseball field. Once you lock up your bikes and secure the gear, it’s a quick five minute walk to the nearby beach access point to Lanikai beach.

#### **Let the Adventure Begin**

Lanikai beach is known for its soft, powdery white sand, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and amazing views of the Mokulua Islands. It is one of the most serene and picturesque beaches in Hawaii and the perfect place to immerse yourself in the beauty of Oahu. Get your snorkel gear ready and head out into the ocean. Remember to use the provided snorkel-mask defog to give you the clearest view possible. Calm, shallow waters, vibrant coral reefs, and diverse sea-life make this beach a snorkeler’s paradise. You’ll have the chance to swim alongside colorful schools of fish, observe sea turtles grazing among the coral, and discover the diverse sea life underneath the water’s surface. For those new to snorkeling, the calm waters provide an easy introduction, while experienced snorkelers will enjoy the variety of sea-life this beach has to offer. When you are done snorkeling, feel free to explore on the beach or relax on the famous powder-soft sand before you are ready to head back. When you are done, head back to our storefront in Kailua where our adventure guides will help you unload your gear before you head off on your next adventure!
