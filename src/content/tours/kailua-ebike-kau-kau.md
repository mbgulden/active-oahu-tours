## Kailua E-Bike Kau Kau Guided Adventure

The best way to experience Kailua? On two wheels, with the wind in your hair and local food in your belly. "Kau kau" means food in Hawaiian pidgin — and on this tour, we take that seriously.

### Why This Tour?

Kailua Town is a food lover's paradise hiding in plain sight. While tourists flock to Waikiki for overpriced resort food, locals know that the real flavors of Oahu are in the neighborhoods. Kailua's food scene blends Hawaiian tradition, Asian influence, and modern island creativity — and on this tour, you'll taste the best of it.

But this isn't just a food tour. It's an e-bike adventure along the windward coast's most scenic routes. Between bites, you'll cruise past Lanikai Beach (consistently ranked one of the world's best), ride the Marsh Trail with panoramic views, and roll through Kailua's charming neighborhoods.

### What to Expect

Your guide meets you at the Active Oahu shop in Kailua Town. After getting fitted for your e-bike and a quick orientation (these bikes are incredibly intuitive — most people feel comfortable in under 5 minutes), you're off.

The route winds through Kailua, with 3–4 food stops carefully chosen for flavor, authenticity, and variety:

**Stop 1 — Classic Hawaiian Plate Lunch.** Two scoops of rice, one scoop of mac salad, and your choice of protein (kalua pig, garlic shrimp, or teriyaki chicken). This is the lunch that built Hawaii.

**Stop 2 — Fresh Poke Bowl.** Ahi tuna, shoyu sauce, sesame, and seaweed over warm rice. Caught that morning. Prepared while you watch. This is what poke is supposed to taste like.

**Stop 3 — Tropical Fruit & Smoothies.** Local papaya, lilikoi, mango, and pineapple. Your guide will teach you how to pick the perfect fruit at a farmers market.

**Stop 4 — Shave Ice (the real stuff).** Not the syrupy tourist version. Real Hawaiian shave ice with local flavors like li hing mui, lilikoi, and haupia cream. The perfect finish.

Between stops, you're riding. The Marsh Trail offers unobstructed views of the Ko'olau Mountains and the ocean. The Lanikai loop takes you past multi-million-dollar beachfront homes and ends at one of the most beautiful beaches on Earth. Throughout the ride, your guide shares stories about Kailua's history, culture, and the people behind the food.
