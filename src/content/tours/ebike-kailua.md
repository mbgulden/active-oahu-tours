## Kailua E-Bike Coastal Explorer

See more of Oahu's windward coast on our effortless e-bike tour. Pedal as much or as little as you like — the electric assist flattens hills and lets you focus on the stunning scenery.

### What to Expect

After a brief orientation on your e-bike, we'll cruise from Kailua Town toward Lanikai — a hidden residential neighborhood home to one of the most beautiful beaches in the world. Stop for photos at Lanikai Beach, then continue along the scenic coastal route.

We'll ride up to the Pali Lookout for jaw-dropping views overlooking Kaneohe Bay and the Ko'olau cliffs, then roll back through Kailua Town with time to explore local boutiques and cafes.

### Tour Schedule

- **Morning Tour**: 8:00 AM – 11:00 AM
- **Afternoon Tour**: 2:00 PM – 5:00 PM
- Available Tuesday–Sunday
