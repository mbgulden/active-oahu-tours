## Kaneohe Bay Kayak & Sandbar Adventure

Discover one of Oahu's most unique natural features — the Kaneohe Sandbar — on this relaxed, family-friendly kayak tour. Protected by one of Hawaii's largest barrier reefs, Kaneohe Bay offers calm, clear waters perfect for paddlers of all skill levels.

### What to Expect

Launch from He'eia State Park and paddle across the tranquil waters of Kaneohe Bay toward the sunken sandbar of Ahu o Laka. At low tide, the sandbar emerges as a white-sand island in the middle of the bay — a surreal experience you won't find anywhere else in Hawaii.

Snorkel the nearby patch reefs, relax on the sandbar, and take in the majestic backdrop of the Ko'olau mountain range and Mokoliʻi Island (Chinaman's Hat).

### Tour Schedule

- **Morning Tour**: 8:00 AM – 12:00 PM
- Available daily (weather permitting)

### Meeting Point

He'eia State Park boat ramp, Kaneohe. Free parking available.
