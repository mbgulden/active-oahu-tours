## Mokulua Islands Kayak Adventure

Experience the best of Oahu's windward coast on our signature kayak tour to the Mokulua Islands. These twin islets — Moku Nui and Moku Iki — are a seabird sanctuary and one of Hawaii's most photographed landmarks.

### What to Expect

After meeting at our Kailua Beach launch point, your guide will fit you with gear and provide a comprehensive safety briefing. The paddle to Moku Nui takes approximately 45-60 minutes depending on conditions.

Once you land, explore the island's tide pools, hike to the summit for panoramic views, and snorkel in the pristine waters surrounding the island. Keep your eyes peeled for honu (Hawaiian green sea turtles)!

### Tour Schedule

- **Morning Tour**: 7:00 AM – 12:00 PM
- **Afternoon Tour**: 1:00 PM – 6:00 PM
- Available daily (weather permitting)

### Meeting Point

Kailua Beach Park boat ramp. Detailed directions provided upon booking.
